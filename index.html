Work

Tata Consultancy Services

Step into the world of innovation and cutting-edge technology as you explore my work experience at Tata Consultancy Services. During my time as a System Engineer from February 2021 to April 2022, I had the privilege of spearheading the modernization of the NEMIS (National Encounter Management Information Systems) project. Guided by a passion for excellence, I fearlessly embraced state-of-the-art technologies such as Spring Boot, Hibernate, Postgre SQL, Redis, Drools, Apache Kafka, and ReactJS. With a focus on efficiency and scalability, I expertly designed and implemented various microservices, leveraging Spring Boot for the backend, Hibernate for ORM, Postgre SQL for data storage, Redis for in-memory caching, and Drools for separating business logic from code.

Furthermore, I seamlessly migrated the legacy Oracle database to a modern PostgreSQL database using data-transfer jobs powered by Apache Spark, ensuring almost zero downtime and service unavailability. This transformative journey showcases my commitment to embracing the latest advancements to create impactful and future-ready solutions.

Magnetar Integrated Solutions Pvt. Ltd

This is a showcase of my journey as a Software Developer at Magnetar Integrated Solutions Pvt. Ltd from January 2019 to February 2021. This chapter of my career was characterized by a relentless pursuit of excellence and a passion for cutting-edge technologies. In collaboration with a dynamic team lead, I played a pivotal role in designing and implementing an orchestrator service that revolutionized the management of all services, leveraging Spring, FaaS, and JWT-based authentication, along with meticulous monitoring using Splunk.

Drawing upon my expertise in Java, SpringBoot, and Spring Data JPA, I crafted a powerful service responsible for loading and transforming incoming data, adeptly incorporating validation checks using Drools before forwarding it to downstream services. Additionally, I undertook the exciting challenge of creating and scheduling complex workflows using Apache Airflow, skillfully handling cross workflow invocations, failure retry scenarios, and eradicating duplicate runs. The mastery of front-end technologies like JavaScript, React, Redux, and Tailwind CSS enabled me to create and maintain a seamless, cross-platform UI, eliminating prop drilling and undesired re-renders with the use of Jotai, Redux, and Tailwind CSS. This exhilarating journey showcases my ability to take on multifaceted projects, driving them to successful fruition with precision and innovation.
